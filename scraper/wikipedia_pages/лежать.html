Russian

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

Inherited from Proto-Slavic *ležati, from Proto-Indo-European *legʰ-.

Pronunciation

[edit]
  • IPA(key): [lʲɪˈʐatʲ]
  • Audio:(file)

Verb

[edit]

лежа́ть • (ležátʹ) impf (perfective лечь or полежа́ть, verbal noun лежа́ние)

  1. to lie (to be in a horizontal position)
    Ма́льчик лежа́л на излече́нии. ― Málʹčik ležál na izlečénii. ― A boy lay in recovery.
  2. to be situated
  3. to rest, to be incumbent
