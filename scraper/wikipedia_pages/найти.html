Russian

[edit]

Alternative forms

[edit]
  • найдти (najdti) — obsolete

Etymology

[edit]

Inherited from Proto-Slavic *najьti (“find”). By surface analysis, на- (na-) +‎ -йти́ (-jtí). Cognates include Ukrainian знайти́ (znajtý), Belarusian знайсці́ (znajscí).

Pronunciation

[edit]
  • IPA(key): [nɐjˈtʲi]
  • IPA(key): [nɐjˈtʲːi] (phonetic respelling: найдти́)
  • Audio:(file)

Verb

[edit]

найти́ • (najtí) pf (imperfective находи́ть)

  1. to find
    • 2005, “Вся моя́ любо́вь [Vsja mojá ljubóvʹ]”, performed by t.A.T.u:
      и себя́ не найдёшь в нелюби́мых дома́х
      и нарвёшься на страх как на ми́нах впотьма́х
      i sebjá ne najdjóšʹ v neljubímyx domáx
      i narvjóšʹsja na strax kak na mínax vpotʹmáx
      And you will not find yourself in an unloved home
      And you will run into fear like into mines in the dark
  2. (figuratively) to think, to consider
  3. to come across
  4. to be located, to be situated
  5. to happen to have

Ukrainian

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

Inherited from Old Ruthenian найти́ (najtí), from Old East Slavic наити́ (naití), from Proto-Slavic *najьti. By surface analysis, на- (na-) +‎ йти́ (jtý)

Pronunciation

[edit]
  • IPA(key): [nɐi̯ˈtɪ]
  • Audio:(file)

Verb

[edit]

найти́ • (najtý) pf (imperfective нахо́дити) (transitive)

  1. to find
  2. to figure out, to come up with, to think of
    Synonym: приду́мати (prydúmaty)
  3. to find, to consider, to think, to see
    Synonyms: вба́чити (vbáčyty), поба́чити (pobáčyty)
  4. (dated, colloquial) to give birth, to bring forth, to have (a child (usually, illegitimate))
    Synonym: нагуля́ти (nahuljáty)

Verb

[edit]

найти́ • (najtý) pf (imperfective нахо́дити) (intransitive)

  1. to overwhelm, to engulf, to wash over, to sweep over, to set in, to kick in, to be struck with [with на (na, + accusative) ‘somebody’]
    Synonyms: оволоді́ти (ovolodíty), охопи́ти (oxopýty), напа́сти (napásty), напли́нути (naplýnuty), нахли́нути (naxlýnuty)
    1. (impersonal) to get into, to come over [with на (na, + accusative) ‘somebody’]
    Не зна́ю, що на ме́не найшло́. ― Ne znáju, ščo na méne najšló. ― I don't know what has gotten into me.
  2. to gather, to congregate, to flock
    Synonyms: згрома́дитися (zhromádytysja), зібра́тися (zibrátysja), зійти́ся (zijtýsja), набі́гти (nabíhty), наплисти́ (naplystý)
    1. to advance on, to attack, to invade [with на (na, + accusative) ‘sth./sb.’]
  3. (usually, of weather phenomena) to approach, to sweep in, to move in, to roll in, to loom
    Synonyms: насу́нутися (nasúnutysja), набли́зитися (nablýzytysja), насу́нути (nasúnuty), підступи́ти (pidstupýty), наплисти́ (naplystý), набі́гти (nabíhty), нахли́нути (naxlýnuty)
