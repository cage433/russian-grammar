Russian

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

Inherited from Proto-Slavic *liti (“to pour”).

Pronunciation

[edit]
  • IPA(key): [lʲitʲ]
  • Audio:(file)

Verb

[edit]

лить • (litʹ) impf (perfective поли́ть or нали́ть)

  1. to pour, to spill
  2. to shed
  3. to cast, to mould, to found
