Russian

[edit]

Etymology 1

[edit]

от- (ot-) +‎ ре́зать (rézatʹ)

Pronunciation

[edit]
  • IPA(key): [ɐˈtrʲezətʲ]

Verb

[edit]

отре́зать • (otrézatʹ) pf (imperfective отреза́ть) (the impf./pf. forms differ in word stress in the infinitive and have different conjugations)

  1. to cut off
    семь раз отме́рь, оди́н отре́жь ― semʹ raz otmérʹ, odín otréžʹ ― look before you leap
    отрезать пути́ ― otrezatʹ putí ― close the door, bar the way
  2. to snip off
  3. to divide, to apportion
  4. (speech) to cut short, to snap out

Etymology 2

[edit]

отре́зать (otrézatʹ) +‎ -ать (-atʹ)

Pronunciation

[edit]
  • IPA(key): [ɐtrʲɪˈzatʲ]

Verb

[edit]

отреза́ть • (otrezátʹ) impf (perfective отре́зать) (the impf./pf. forms differ in word stress in the infinitive and have different conjugations)

  1. to cut off
    семь раз отме́рь, оди́н — отре́жь ― semʹ raz otmérʹ, odín — otréžʹ ― look before you leap
    отрезать пути́ ― otrezatʹ putí ― close the door, bar the way
  2. to snip off
  3. to divide, to apportion
  4. (speech) to cut short, to snap out
