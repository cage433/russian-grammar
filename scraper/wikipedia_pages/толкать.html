Russian

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

See толкнуть (tolknutʹ).

Pronunciation

[edit]
  • IPA(key): [tɐɫˈkatʲ]
  • Audio:(file)

Verb

[edit]

толка́ть • (tolkátʹ) impf (perfective толкну́ть)

  1. to push, to shove, to thrust
  2. to incite, to instigate
  3. (slang) to sell
