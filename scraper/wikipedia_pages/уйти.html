Russian

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

у- (u-) +‎ -йти́ (-jtí)

Pronunciation

[edit]
  • IPA(key): [ʊjˈtʲi]
  • IPA(key): [ʊjˈtʲːi] (phonetic respelling: уйдти́)
  • Audio:(file)

Verb

[edit]

уйти́ • (ujtí) pf (imperfective уходи́ть)

  1. to leave, to depart, to go (away) (by foot)
  2. to escape, to evade
  3. (colloquial) to resign, to retire
  4. to be lost
  5. to fail
  6. to sink, to plunge
  7. to be spent
  8. (figuratively, poetic) to die
